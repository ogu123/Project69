Places that you must visit

McDonalds

I had visted McDonalds on 19th of July 2022

McDonalds was about 2 miles away from my house. I ate 20 McNuggets on my own and I went with my mum, dad and two brothers. I also tried an Oreo McFlurry for the first time and it tasted pretty good. The food was fantastic and I was served very well by the people who were serving there. The car ride to and from the restaurant was also very fun as we played music and sung along with it.
